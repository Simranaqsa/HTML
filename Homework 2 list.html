Prime Minister of India

1. Pandit Jawaharlal Nehru
  1. Born: 14 November 1889, Prayagraj
  2. Parents: Motilal Nehru, Swarup Rani Nehru
  3. Spouse: Kamala Nehru (m. 1916–1936)
  4. Children: Indira Gandhi
  5. Died: 27 May 1964, Teen Murti Bhavan, New Delhi
2. Gulzarilal Nanda
  1. Born: 4 July 1898, Sialkot, Pakistan
  2. Party: Indian National Congress
  3. Spouse: Lakshmi Nanda
  4. Previous offices: Acting Prime Minister of India (1966–1966)
  5. Awards: Bharat Ratna, Padma Vibhushan
3. Lal Bahudur Shastri
  1. Born: 2 October 1904, Mughalsarai
  2. Died: 11 January 1966, Tashkent, Uzbekistan
  3. Spouse: Lalita Shastri (m. 1928–1966)
  4. Children: Anil Shastri, Sunil Shastri, Hari Krishna Shastri, Suman Shastri, Ashok Shastri,Kusum Shastri
  5. Education: Mahatma Gandhi Kashi Vidyapith (1925), Harish Chandra Post Graduate College Varanasi
4. Indira Gandhi
  1. Born: 19 November 1917, Prayagraj
  2. Daughter of Jawaharlal Nehru
  3. Assassinated: 31 October 1984, New Delhi
  4. Spouse: Feroze Gandhi (m. 1942–1960)
  5. Education: Somerville College, St. Mary's Convent Inter College
  6. Children: Rajiv Gandhi, Sanjay Gandhi
5. Morarji Desai
  1. Born: 29 February 1896, Valsad
  2. Children: Kanti Desai
  3. Party: Janata Party
  4. Awards: Nishan-e-Pakistan, Bharat Ratna
  5. Died: 10 April 1995, Mumbai
6. Charan Singh
  • Born: 23 December 1902, Hapur
  • Education: Dr. Bhimrao Ambedkar University, Agra College Agra
  • Parents: Mir Singh, Netar Kaur
  • Children: Ajit Singh, Satyavati Solanki, Sharda Singh, Gyanvati Singh, Saroj Verma, Vedvati Singh
  • Died: 29 May 1987, New Delhi
7. Rajiv Gandhi
  • Born: 20 August 1944, Mumbai
  • Education: Trinity College
  • Awards: Bharat Ratna, Indira Gandhi Prize
  • Son of Indira Gandhi & Feroze Gandhi
  • Date of assassination: 21 May 1991
8. Vishwanath Pratap Singh
  • Born: 25 June 1931, Prayagraj
  • Education: Col. Brown Cambridge School
  • Previous offices: Prime Minister of India (1989–1990)
  • Children: Ajeya Pratap Singh, Abhai Singh
  • Died: 27 November 2008, Apollo Hospital
9. Chandra Shekhar Singh
  • Born: 1 July 1927, Ibrahimpatti
  • Party: Congress Socialist Party
  • Spouse: Duja Devi
  • Children: Neeraj Shekhar, Pankaj Shekhar Singh
  • Died: 8 July 2007, Apollo Hospital, Indraprastha
